Los Angeles — Individually numbered, limited-edition eyewear crafted at the intersection of history, mythology and desire.

Founded in 2014 by French designer Jérôme Mage, Jacques Marie Mage creates limited-edition eyewear defined by bold design, rare craftsmanship, and cultural expression. Individually numbered and handcrafted in Japan using refined materials like cured acetate and titanium, each frame is produced in highly exclusive quantities — making every piece a collectible object of modern luxury.
