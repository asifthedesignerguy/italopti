About
Thom Browne.

Founded in New York in 2001, Thom Browne has become one of the most singular voices in American fashion — redefining the suit, deconstructing tradition, and building a visual language of rigorous, institutional beauty. The eyewear collection is an extension of this vision: frames designed with the same discipline as a bespoke jacket.

Available in optical and sun, the collection spans classic acetate frames, titanium wire constructions, and experimental sculptural forms — each produced to the highest manufacturing standards in Japan.
