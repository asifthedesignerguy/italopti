ItalOpti is a premium eyewear distribution company established in India in 2025 through the partnership between OptiItalia Group, Dubai, and Optimed Corporation, Mumbai.
The company was created to develop and distribute a curated portfolio of premium, luxury, and leading independent eyewear brands across the Indian market, with a strong focus on brand positioning, selective distribution, and long-term value creation

The Founders

50+ years.
One vision.

The founding team brings together the expertise of Mauro Casotto and Gian Paolo Biasotto from OptiItalia Group, alongside Ameet Poojara and Mukul Poojara from Optimed Corporation. Together, they bring more than 50 years of experience in the eyewear industry, combining international brand-building expertise with deep-rooted market relationships.

ItalOpti was founded on a clear belief: the right brands require the right distribution. We represent a carefully curated portfolio of eyewear houses whose values, design language, and commercial positioning demand thoughtful market development rather than mass distribution, partnering with retailers who can deliver the visibility, service standards, and brand experience each collection deserves.
