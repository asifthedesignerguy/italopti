ItalOpti is a premium eyewear distribution company established in India in 2025 through the partnership between OptiItalia Group, Dubai, and Optimed Corporation, Mumbai.
The company was created to develop and distribute a curated portfolio of premium, luxury, and leading independent eyewear brands across the Indian market, with a strong focus on brand positioning, selective distribution, and long-term value creation

The Founders

50+ years.
One vision.

The founding team brings together the experience of Mauro Casotto and Gian Paolo Biasotto from OptiItalia Group, together with Ameet Poojara and Mukul Poojara from Optimed Corporation.

Together, the two groups bring more than 50 years of experience in the eyewear industry, combining international brand-building expertise with long-established market relationships in India.
